About Me

Smruti is a meticulous designer who has a passion for typography, editorial design, brand identity and illustration. She has designed projects for live-clients including the City of Cambridge, and a non-profit organization ‘Education for Change’ which was part of Creative Day for Social Good. Smruti has excellent management, organizational, and communication skills and is proficient with several applications in the Adobe Creative Suite as well as Microsoft Office.
